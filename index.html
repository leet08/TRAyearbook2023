    • The Year of 2023

    The year of 2022-2023 was an unforgettable year as we did so many fun and interesting things! This year started with Dr. Rebekah and P. Paul Kim reopening the TRA (The Rivendell Academy) homeschool. TRA is a safe haven where we can learn not only academics, but also spiritual knowledge and understanding. Dr. Rebekah and P. Paul, the founders of our church and of TRA, wanted to shield us from the bad influence of the dark outside world. Inside our school, we have wonderful teachers that teach not only the subjects, but also the spiritual matters that are going on. P. Donald provided us with his house for us, so that we can learn and play there. Parents have volunteered to cook food for us that fill our bellies. But they not only fill our bellies for physical food, but also provide us with spiritual meals.

    Our School Story

    Our school was founded by P. Paul and Dr. Rebekah Kim, who are the founders of Antioch Baptist Church. Its origin dates from 2012 in Dilli, Timor-Leste. In September, 2022, Dr. Rebekah reopened The Rivendell Acadamy in Boston to the kids of Antioch Baptist Church. The Rivendell Acadamy is a thriving homeschool with 12 students and 23 amazing teachers. TRA teaches 3rd to 8th grade. The Rivendell Acadamy gets its name from J.R.R. Tolkien's The Lord of the Rings. In his book, Rivendell is the beautiful home of the elves where everything is peaceful and strong while everywhere else in Middle Earth is full of fighting and danger. The Rivendell Acadamy is made as a safe place for children to develop in knowledge and character on the foundation of Christ, so that they will stand firm as they go on to the future.
